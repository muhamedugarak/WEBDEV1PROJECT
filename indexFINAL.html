Moe's Repair and Performance

Welcome to Moes Repair and Performance. I have experience with German cars since I was a young kid, watching and helping my brothers with their cars. I started wrenching on my own at the age of twelve. I have experience with Volkswagen and its subsidiaries, Mercedes E and S classes, BMW (especially with the N52 and the N54 twin turbo engines, and vehicles that used their drivetrains and control units. We are all ASE certified with a passion for cars. We love what we do and take great joy in the work that we perform. Quality and honesty is guaranteed.

We specialize in maintenance in the aforementioned vehicles and have experience with tuning BMW's through MHD and JB4. Once a tune is chosen through JB4, it will be able to customize and fine tune road and race maps through MHD. You can choose what grade gasoline you would like to keep your vehicle running on, unless you prefer switchable maps.

We also employ mechanics experienced in other European and domestic models, and can outsource jobs in differing specializations such as transmission rebuilds and ECU resoldering. We provide virtually any service that is asked of through qualified and reputable services.
